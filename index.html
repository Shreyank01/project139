AI Ping Pong







Instructions

  1. Keep your pc/mobile/laptop stright.

  2. Take your left wrist up and down to move the slider
    Note :The red dot must be appear when you move your wrist.

  3. Press 'Play' button to start the game.

  4. Hit the ball by the slider to bounce it back.

  5. The speed will increase when you hit the ball.

  6. if you lose opponent(computer) will score the point
    and if computer lose you will score a point.

  7. Enjoy the game
